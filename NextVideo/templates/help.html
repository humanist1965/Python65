NextVideo JSON API

Methods:

/Series
Returns a list of all the Series
/WatchList
Returns a list your WatchList
/Series/{seriesID}
Get next episode to watch and increment
/Series//Play
Play next episode (update datetime watched)
/Series/{seriesID}/Inc
Manually increment to the next episode
/Series/{seriesID}/Dec
Manually decrement to the previous episode
/Series/{seriesID}/IncSeason
Manually increment to the next Season
/Series/{seriesID}/DecSeason
Manually decrement to the previous Season
